01. How we make S-K-Microsource products
How we make S-K-Microsource products
S-K-Microsource (SKM) is mined from mineral deposits and screened to size 1/8 or 1/16 minus, called SKM AG, to create amendment applications.
We grind (mill) the SKM ore into a powder at 4 to 8 inches to produce 44 to 10-micron size solution-grade particles called Ultra Fine (SKM UF) to create a liquid amendment.
