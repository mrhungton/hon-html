Scope of usage

Our organic mineral products are applied to many fields, bringing high economic benefits to users and human values to society.
Agricultural crops

S-K-Microsource® is a soil improver containing elements present in the soil that are beneficial to plant growth. SKM will help to release the locked-in nutrients in the agricultural land, such as calcium, for easier plant uptake. Crops using S-K-M in combination with organic farming methods will increase yields and reduce input costs for you.
S-K-Microsource® helps crops with increased fruit, vegetable, and flower size, faster growth, and deeper color. Plants have healthy and more evenly developed roots.

Animal Nutrition

Micronutrients in small quantities in S-K-Microsource® are essential for animal growth and immune function, suitable in both cattle and poultry.
The observations indicate that the mineral composition of S-K-Microsource® is a beneficial supplement that improves the growth performance and health of cattle and poultry.

Aquaculture

S-K-Microsource® helps aquatic animals with good resistance in all freshwater, brackish, and saltwater bodies. It supports cleaning the water environment to help aquatic animals grow well and develop healthily.
S-K-M products are suitable for sustainable aquaculture, environmental protection, and harmonization with other economic sectors.

Revitalize the land and water

Soil and water reclamation is a process of removing contaminants from soil and water.

Treat odor pollution

Our product has proven to reduce Ammonia in poultry houses which will result in healthier birds.
